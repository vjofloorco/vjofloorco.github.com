Tile

Ceramic and porcelain tile can bring elegance to your home. There are numerous styles of ceramic tile, and there are many unique ways to install it. Tile can be installed on the floor or on the wall in patterns. Either way you install it, it has easy maintenance and may be the last floor you will ever need to put down.

Vallejo Floor Company also sells Avaire Pocelean Tile. This revolutionary new product allows the tile to be clicked together over an existing subfoor without the need for the costly preparations associated with traditional tile installation.
